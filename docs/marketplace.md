The Ragora Marketplace is where knowledge becomes a product. Experts publish their curated workspaces — legal templates, API documentation, research papers, community archives — and other users or AI agents can search and retrieve from them. Sellers earn revenue on every access; buyers get instant, AI-searchable knowledge without building it themselves.

## Overview

The Marketplace has two sides:

- **As a Buyer**: Discover and access high-quality workspaces curated by domain experts. Search them via the API, MCP, or chat — just like your own workspaces.
- **As a Seller**: Monetize your expertise by publishing workspaces with subscription, one-time, or pay-per-use pricing. Earn revenue when AI agents or users retrieve from your content.

## Buying Products

### Finding Products

1. Go to **Marketplace** from the dashboard
2. Browse or search for products
3. View product details including:
   - Description and topics covered
   - Document count
   - Pricing (one-time, subscription, or per-use)
   - Seller information

### Purchase Options

| Type | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **One-Time** | Pay once, access forever | Stable reference material |
| **Subscription** | Monthly fee for ongoing access | Frequently updated content |
| **Per-Use** | Pay per query/retrieval | Occasional access |

### After Purchase

Once you buy a product:

1. It appears in your **Workspaces** list
2. Query it via API, workspace chat, MCP, or bots (Discord, Slack, Telegram)
3. Access persists based on your purchase type

## Selling Products

Turn your knowledge into revenue by creating and selling products.

### Getting Started

1. Go to **Workspaces** <a className="btn-inline" href="https://ragora.app/kb">Workspaces &rarr;</a>, open a workspace
2. Click the publish/marketplace option to list your workspace as a product
3. Configure your product:
   - Name and description
   - Category
   - Set pricing (Free, One-Time, or Subscription)

### Pricing Your Product

**One-Time Purchase:**
- Buyers pay once for permanent access
- Good for reference material, courses, archives

**Subscription:**
- Buyers pay monthly for continued access
- Good for frequently updated content, news, research

**Per-Use:**
- Buyers pay per retrieval
- You set the price per use
- Good for high-value specialized knowledge

### Making Your Product Discoverable

**Write a clear description:**
- What knowledge does it contain?
- Who is it useful for?
- What questions can it answer?

**Set appropriate pricing:**
- Research similar products
- Consider your audience
- Start lower to build reviews, raise later

## Revenue & Payouts

### How Earnings Work

When someone purchases your product:

1. Payment is processed via Stripe
2. Platform fees are deducted (20% + $0.50/transaction)
3. Your revenue is added to your seller balance

### Revenue Split

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Sale Price | 100% |
| Platform Fee | -20% |
| Transaction Fee | -$0.50 |
| **Your Revenue** | 80% - $0.50 |

**Example:** $10 sale → $10 - $2.00 - $0.50 = **$7.50** to you

### How You Get Paid

**One-time and subscription sales** go directly to your connected Stripe account — no action needed.

**Per-use earnings** (microtransactions) accumulate and can be withdrawn from **Settings** once you reach the minimum threshold.

### Setting Up Stripe Connect

To receive payouts, connect your Stripe account:

1. Go to **Settings** <a className="btn-inline" href="https://ragora.app/settings">Settings &rarr;</a>
2. Click **Connect Stripe Account**
3. Complete Stripe's onboarding
4. Your bank account is now linked

## Product Management

### Updating Your Product

1. Go to **Workspaces** <a className="btn-inline" href="https://ragora.app/kb">Workspaces &rarr;</a>
2. Open the workspace associated with your product
3. Edit description, pricing, or category from the settings
4. Click **Save Changes**

Existing buyers keep their access. New pricing applies to new purchases.

### Adding Content

To add more knowledge to a product:

1. Go to the workspace assigned to your product
2. Upload new documents or connect sources
3. New content is automatically available to buyers

### Viewing Analytics

See how your product is performing:

- **Views**: How many people viewed your product page
- **Purchases**: Number of buyers
- **Queries**: How often buyers search your product
- **Revenue**: Total earnings from this product

## Buyer Protection

### What Buyers Get

- Access to all documents in the product
- Updates when the seller adds new content
- Support via Ragora for access issues

### Refund Policy

Marketplace purchases are non-refundable. Review product descriptions carefully before purchasing.

## FAQ

### Can I sell my own collections?

Yes! Any workspace you own can be turned into a marketplace product.

### What happens if I delete a product?

Existing buyers retain their access (especially one-time purchases). The product is removed from the marketplace for new buyers.

### Can I offer free products?

Yes. Set the price to $0 or select "Free" as the purchase type.

### How do I handle support questions?

Buyers can contact you through the product page. Respond within 48 hours to maintain good seller standing.
